 6      SAGA-Bliss is a light-weight Python package that implements parts of the U{OGF

 8      interface specification and provides plug-ins for different 
 9      distributed middleware systems and services. SAGA-Bliss implements the most 
10      commonly used features of GFD.90 based upon extensive use-case analysis, and 
11      focuses on usability and simple deployment in real-world heterogeneous 
12      distributed computing environments and application scenarios.
